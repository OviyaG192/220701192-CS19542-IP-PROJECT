Climate Summit: Nations Agree on New Carbon Targets

In a groundbreaking agreement at the latest Climate Summit, world leaders committed to reducing carbon emissions. The goal is to achieve net-zero emissions by 2050, with interim targets for 2030 and 2040. The agreement focuses on renewable energy adoption, deforestation control, and reducing fossil fuel reliance.
